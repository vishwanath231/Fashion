september 27, 2020 / 2 min read

The notion of the global fashion industry is a product of the modern age. Proir to the mid-19th century, most clothing was custom-made. It was handmade for individuals, either as home production or on order from dressmakers and tailors. By the beginning of the 20th century - with the rise of new technologies such as the sewing machine, the rise of global capitalism and the development of the factory sysytem of production, and the proliferation of retail outlets such as department stores - clothing had increasing come to be mass - production in standard sizes and sold at fixed prices.


Although the fashion industry developed first in Europe and America, as of 2017, it is an international and highly globalized in one country manfactured in another, and sold worldwide.
